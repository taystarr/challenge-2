About Me

I am a Florida native that started off my career in the medical field and moved to DC mid-pandemic. I graduated from Florida SouthWestern State College with my nursing degree in 2017 and have since been a pediatric critical care nurse. My love for coding started as a reprieve from working in a hospital during the pandemic and has since blossomed into something I genuinely enjoy and look forward to.
I started the coding bootcamp offered through George Washington University in February 2022, where we cover a mix of front-end and back-end web development skills including Git, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, Express.js, SQL, PWAs, and more. My anticipated graduation date is July 2022.
