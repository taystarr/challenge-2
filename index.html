About Me

I am Florida born and recently moved to the DC area. I started off my career in the medical field. I graduated from Florida SouthWestern State College with my nursing degree in 2017 and have since been a pediatric critical care nurse. My love for coding started as a reprieve from working in a hospital during the pandemic and has blossomed into something I genuinely enjoy and look forward to. I started a coding bootcamp offered through George Washington University where we cover a mix of front end and back end web development skills including working with Git, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, Express.js, SQL, PWAs, and more. My anticipated graduation from the program is in late July.
